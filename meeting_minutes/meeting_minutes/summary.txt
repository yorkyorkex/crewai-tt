In the second quarter of 2023, FinTech Plus reported a significant revenue increase of 25%, reaching $125 million, with a solid gross profit margin of 58%. The company's EBITDA rose to $37.5 million, indicating a 30% margin, and net income increased by 60% compared to the previous year. The expansion of the high-yield savings product line and RoboAdvisor platform contributed to the growth of the total addressable market. FinTech Plus has diversified its securities portfolio, investing in corporate bonds and maintaining a healthy balance sheet with total assets at $1.5 billion and liabilities at $900 million. The debt to equity ratio remains robust at 1.5, with customer acquisition costs decreasing while lifetime value continues to grow. The company anticipates revenue of $135 million in the next quarter and plans for the IPO of its subsidiary, Pay Plus, expected to raise $200 million. Overall, FinTech Plus maintains a positive outlook for future growth.
